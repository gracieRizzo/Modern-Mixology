Cocktails A-M

The American Dream

  • 1 oz PAMA Pomegranate Liquer
  • 1 oz black cherry rum
  • 1 1/2 oz POM Wonderful pomegranate cherry juice
  • 1/2 oz fresh sour mix
  • white fondant stars for garnish
  • blueberries for garnish

Bloody Mary

  • 3 oz tomato juice
  • 1 1/2 oz vodka
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • 1 dash of Worcestershire sauce
  • Celery salt
  • Ground pepper
  • Hot pepper sauce to taste
  • Celery stalk and/or pickle spear for garnish
  • Lemon and/or lime wedge for garnish

Central Park

  • 2 oz New Amsterdam Gin
  • 3 drops of vanilla extract
  • 4 oz tomato juice
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • pinch of cayenne pepper
  • vanilla bean for garnish
  • cherry tomato for garnish

The Devil's Punch

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz orange liqueur
  • 1 oz limoncello
  • 1 oz sour mix
  • Dash of orange juice

El Presidente

  • 2 oz light rum
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz pineapple juice
  • dash of grenadine

The Firefly

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz grapefruit juice
  • 1 oz grenadine

Georgia On My Mind

  • 2 oz Four Roses Bourbon
  • 0.5 oz Domaine de Canton Ginger Liqueur
  • 1.5 oz peach nectar (Looza brand)
  • 2-3 dashes of Bitter Truth Celery Bitters
  • basil

Hot Apple Pie

  • 2 oz Tuaca
  • hot apple cider
  • Whipped cream
  • Cinnamon stick for garnish

Irish Car Bomb

  • 3/4 pint Guinness
  • 1/2 shot Irish cream liqueur
  • 1/2 shot Irish whiskey

Japanese Slipper

  • 1 oz Midori melon liqueur
  • oz Cointreau orange liqueur
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • honeydew melon slice for garnish

Kamikaze

  • 1 1/2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • Lime wedge for garnish

Lava Lamp Martini

  • 2 oz vodka or citrus vodka
  • 1 oz raspberry liqueur
  • 1 oz honey

Mai Tai

  • 1 oz light rum
  • 1 oz dark rum
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz orange curacao
  • 1/2 oz orgeat syrup
  • Maraschino cherry for garnish
